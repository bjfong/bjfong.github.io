More About Me


Software engineer. Food lover/Yelp Elite. Sports fanatic. TV show binger. Amateur adventure seeker.

My name is Brian. I was born and raised in the Bay Area, graduated from the University of California, San Diego in 2016 with a B.S. in Computer Science, and now I am currently working as a software engineer in Los Angeles. I am a huge sports fan (Go Lakers!), I love food, and I watch a lot of TV shows.

One of my favorite things in life is food. Food is inanimate but at the same time can be so expressive. Food can be used in light of good news and it can also be used in light of bad news. Food has many uses but most importantly of all: it just tastes damn good.

Because I am an amateur traveler, combining that with my love for food means a lot of adventures and exploring. I love the big, popular restaurants and I also love the hole-in-the-wall restaurants. Both of these types of restaurants tell you a lot about a city and it's my goal to learn as much as I can about the places I travel to through their food as much as through their history. At the end of the day, food is a result of a place's history. Food transforms generation by generation and I want to taste a place's history through the first bite.
