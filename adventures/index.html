More About Me


Software engineer. Food lover. Sports fanatic.
TV show binger. Amateur traveler/adventure seeker.

My name is Brian. I was born and raised in the Bay Area, graduated from the University of California, San Diego in 2016 with a B.S. in Computer Science, and now I am currently working as a software engineer in Los Angeles. I am a huge sports fan (Go Lakers!), I love food, and I watch a lot of TV shows.

I haven't gotten the chance to travel in my life so far. I didn't go on summer trips or any study abroad programs, so I don't have many memories and adventures overseas or even in other parts of the USA. Now that I am making (enough) money, I'm taking advantage of this great time in my life to see what the world has to offer. This page has all the information about my travels: big or small, domestic or international, day trips or week-long adventures. Check out where I've been and join me on this adventure!
