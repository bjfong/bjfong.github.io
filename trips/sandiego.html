What I Disliked

Tips

  • The food. Tacos el Gordo, Phil's, Oscar's, Bahn Thai, Vallarta. This is our tier one list of places to go and you need to go. Gordos, Phil's, and Oscar's are consistently on Yelp's Top 100 restaurants lists. Bahn Thai and Vallarta are San Diego local secrets. You're welcome.

Final Thoughts

I call this summer the best summer of my life, and I hope you know why now. When I'm older, I'll think back to this summer as THE summer to remember. I'll tell my grandkids about this summer. I still look back at pictures sometimes because I just miss it that much. Everything was just perfect. I'll remember this summer for the rest of my life and this website will help me preserve those memories.
